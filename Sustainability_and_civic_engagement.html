The trial of King Kong

This is an interactive drama designed to raise awareness of sexual and gender-based violence. It recounts real-life situations of abuse and offers an interactive analysis of the legal assessment of the facts and the penalties incurred. This play has given me a better understanding of certain harmful behaviours and the type of legal procedings imposed on these perpetrators.

The climate mural

This supervised activity aims to raise awareness of current climate and environmental issues through a card game. The idea is to link these cards together in groups to stimulate reflection. This activity allowed me to synthesise my knowledge of ecology and connect the dots.
